07 — FAQ
---------
Q: How does per-slide pricing work?
A: You pick a product and a design tier, then set the number of slides. The price per slide is fixed by tier, so your total is transparent before you order — no hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

Q: What formats do you deliver?
A: Fully editable PowerPoint and Keynote as standard, plus Google Slides and print-ready PDF on request. Master templates ship with a documented style guide.

Q: How do reviews work?
A: You review online and comment directly on every page, pixel-precise. Each comment and approval is documented in one place — no email chaos, no version confusion.

Q: Do you sign NDAs?
A: Yes. Full confidentiality is standard, and we counter-sign your NDA on request before any material is shared. Source files and drafts stay private to your project.

Q: How do international payments and invoicing work?
A: We invoice in EUR via SEPA and accept USD. You receive compliant international invoices suitable for Switzerland, Germany, the US and LATAM. Standard terms are 50% down, 50% on approval.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied?
A: Your tier includes fixed revision cycles, and we always design one style sample first so the direction is approved before full production. If the sample isn't right, you don't proceed — and you can add extra revision cycles anytime.
